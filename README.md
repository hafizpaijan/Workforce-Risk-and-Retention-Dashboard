# Workforce-Risk-and-Retention-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project delivers a comprehensive, interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to provide strategic insights into employee attrition patterns, retention risks, and key turnover drivers.

**Objective:** To empower HR managers and business leaders with data-driven tools to proactively identify high-risk employee segments, understand the root causes of turnover, and inform targeted retention strategies.

## Key Insights & Business Impact

The analysis, driven by the metrics shown in the dashboard, reveals critical patterns that demand immediate attention:

* **Elevated Attrition Risk:** The overall **Attrition Rate is 16.1%**, which falls within the typical 15%-20% range for the Tech industry but indicates a significant cost and talent loss concern.
* **Highest Risk Age Group:** The **26-35 age group** accounts for the highest volume of attrition (**116 exits**), suggesting a failure to retain employees during their prime career growth stage.
* **Salary Sensitivity:** The majority of attrition (**163 exits**) occurs in the **"Upto 5K" Salary Slab**, indicating that compensation may be a primary lever for retention improvement, particularly at entry-to-mid levels.
* **Job Role Focus:** **Laboratory Technician** and **Sales Executive** roles demonstrate the highest total attrition, demanding specific attention from department heads.
* **Gender Disparity:** **Male employees (64% of attrition)** show a disproportionately higher turnover rate compared to Female employees (36%).

The final interactive dashboard provides the following key views and analytical breakdowns, as visualized above:
